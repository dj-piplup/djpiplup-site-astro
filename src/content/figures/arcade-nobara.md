This one was one of my earliest figures I won from an arcade. I haven't watched past the first episode of JJK so I've never seen her in the show, but I do appreciate that she's got a good not-overdesigned look. Fun fact, when I won this figure, I actually accidentally hooked a bar instead of the figure, and it shook 2 figures off of the back and into the prize hole. I gave the other one to my brother.
